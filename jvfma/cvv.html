For your online safety and security, we require that you enter your credit or debit card verification number (CVV) before we can process your payment.

As shown below, the Card Verification Number (CVV) is the last three digit number printed on the signature panel located on the back of your card.

The CVV is a security feature that allows Yum Juices and your credit card issuer to identify you as the cardholder and provide you with additional security against fraud.
